About Us

The Trio Jerseys was born from a simple belief: every Pakistani football fan deserves to wear their passion with pride.

Our Story

The Trio Jerseys started in 2023 when three friends from Lahore realised how difficult it was to find authentic, high-quality football jerseys in Pakistan. The market was flooded with low-grade replicas that fell apart after a few washes, while genuine player-version jerseys were either unavailable or priced beyond reach.

We decided to change that. Leveraging our combined experience in textile sourcing and e-commerce, we built relationships with trusted manufacturers who produce the same quality jerseys worn by professional players. Our mission was simple: bring premium football jerseys to Pakistani fans at prices that make sense.

What started as a small Instagram page has grown into one of Pakistan’s most trusted jersey stores, serving thousands of customers from Gwadar to Gilgit. Every order we ship carries the same passion that drives fans to cheer for their teams on matchday.

Our Mission

We exist to make every Pakistani football fan feel like a champion. We believe that wearing your team’s jersey is more than just clothing — it’s a statement of belonging, a badge of loyalty, and a connection to a global community of millions.

We are committed to offering only player-version quality jerseys that match the exact specifications of what players wear on the pitch. From the stitching to the fabric weight to the placement of sponsor logos, every detail matters to us because we know it matters to you.

Beyond quality, we are driven by trust. Cash on delivery, transparent pricing, responsive customer support, and a no-questions-asked exchange policy are not just features — they are the foundation of everything we do.

Why We Started

Pakistan has one of the most passionate football fanbases in the world. From the streets of Lyari to the grounds of Islamabad, football runs deep in our culture. Yet for years, Pakistani fans had to settle for subpar merchandise or pay exorbitant international shipping fees to get a decent jersey.

We saw a gap and we filled it. The Trio Jerseys was created to serve the Pakistani football community specifically — with local payment methods like JazzCash and EasyPaisa, nationwide delivery via trusted courier partners, and sizing guides tailored to the South Asian physique.

Every time a customer opens their package and sees the quality of our jerseys, we feel we’ve done our job. That moment of excitement, that pride of wearing your team’s colors — that’s why we do what we do.

The Trio Family

The name “Trio” represents the three founding members who brought this vision to life. Each of us brings a different strength to the table: one handles sourcing and quality control, another manages operations and logistics, and the third drives our marketing and customer experience.

But the Trio family extends beyond the founders. It includes our dedicated customer support team, our warehouse staff who carefully pack every order, the courier partners who deliver across the country, and most importantly — you, our customers. Every order, every review, every social media tag brings us closer together as a community.

When you wear a Trio Jerseys product, you’re not just wearing a shirt. You’re representing a community of Pakistani football lovers who refuse to settle for anything less than the best.

Football Culture In Pakistan

Football in Pakistan is not just a sport — it’s a way of life. From the narrow lanes of Karachi where children play with rolled-up socks as balls, to the packed cafés showing Champions League nights, the beautiful game unites us like nothing else.

We celebrate this culture every day. Whether you support Real Madrid or Barcelona, Brazil or Argentina, Manchester City or Liverpool, the football community in Pakistan transcends club rivalries. We gather to watch matches, debate tactics, and share the highs and lows of the season together.

At The Trio Jerseys, we don’t just sell merchandise. We celebrate the culture that makes football the world’s game. We are fans first, and that understanding shapes everything from the products we choose to the way we talk to our customers. Because at the end of the day, we’re all part of the same global football family.
